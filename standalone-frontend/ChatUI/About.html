Who is Captain Finn?

Meet CAPTAIN FINN, our beloved mascot IEEE FISAT SB with the motto "Think Green For The Globe". Since 2021, CAPTAIN FINN has been dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability and making the Earth greener. CAPTAIN FINN is now proudly reintroduced as the mascot for our chatbot, Finn AI. With renewed energy, CAPTAIN FINN continues to inspire and to guide us in the digital realm!!

About FINN-AI

Our chatbot, FINN AI, is the personalized AI Chatbot of IEEE FISAT SB. It is designed to offer you immediate assistance, address your queries, and guide you through our services seamlessly. Whether you seek information, require support, or simply want to have a chat, our cherished SB mascot, Captain Finn, is here to assist you. The AI chatbot is accessible 24/7 to provide instant support whenever you need it. With a user-friendly interface, interacting with our AI chatbot is easy and natural. Our aim is to ensure that you receive the best possible assistance, and we are always here to support you. Happy chatting!
